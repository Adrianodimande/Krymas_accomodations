Discover the Ponta do Ouro Complex

Exclusive accommodations and a unique space for your experience.

The Ponta do Ouro Complex offers high-standard infrastructure with 4 fully equipped T2 houses and 7 exclusive suites. With a private pool, bar, and gardens, we guarantee the best experience for our guests.

Additionally, we have an excellent location, right next to the main road and in the heart of Ponta do Ouro, close to all local amenities.

What We Offer

In addition to high-quality accommodations, our complex offers a variety of activities and attractions to ensure a complete experience.

Natural Wealth

Explore the surrounding nature with trails and spectacular views. The natural beauty of Ponta do Ouro is unique and enchanting.

Private Pool

Relax and enjoy the complex's private pool, with a peaceful environment perfect for leisure and relaxation.

Local Gastronomy

Delight in the local cuisine. Nearby restaurants and bars offer fresh dishes and unique flavors from the region.

Exclusive Beach

Just a few minutes away, discover the paradisiacal beaches of Ponta do Ouro, perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Outdoor Adventures

For adventure lovers, the region offers activities like hiking, diving, and boat tours. Fun is guaranteed!
